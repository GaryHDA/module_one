Objectives
Goals
  • Relate aquatic litter in our local environment to the global problem.
  • Create a benchmark that clearly establishes the trash levels in our environment for a given time frame.
  • Develop a platform that facilitates open research by all those curious about the world around them.
Objectives
  • Identify a common protocol for quantifying and cataloging beach-litter
  • Develop an understanding of aquatic litter in relation to municipal waste sytems and urban planning
  • Use technology to create an efficient method to catalog and document litter levels
  • Create links/networks with others interested in the subject
  • Apply current methods of data visualisation and analysis to sample results
  • Accomplish all of the above with the least amount of financial burden
Methods
  • Litterature review of all topics associated with the goals and objectives
  • Sampling of litter at a variety of locations
  • Documentation of results
  • If possible engage local authorities and experts
