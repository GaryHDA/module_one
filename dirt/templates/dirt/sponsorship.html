Sponsorhip Costs:
The sponsorship for one beach per year is 3600 CHF.
This includes:

  • 12 beach inventories
  • End of year report
  • Presence in social media
  • Logo associated with the beach

This covers: salaries to pick-up, sort, dispose of trash and enter data, including equipment and transportation costs for the "crew". A portion of that is used to pay the developers salary(the developer also does liiter-inventories), server costs and adminstrative functions.

We also consult with experts in the field of mathematics and solid waste engineering. A portion of the sponsorhip fee makes it possible to develop analytical models and pursue activites through "hammerdirt labs"

If you would like more details we are available to explain our goals and how we intend to achieve them, send an email:

Contact roger[at]hammerdirt.ch

How many litter inventories in one year?
We can do between 7-10 invetories per week

The size of the beach plays a role and also the amount of trash. Picking it up is quick. However sorting it takes the most time. We have done this hundreds of times, we have developed methods and roles within our team that speeds up the process.

Initially the identification of items was the most time consuming. But once we could associate codes with objects, the data entry became a bottleneck. We have automated the process by building an app(this app), the data is entered once and everything is updated. There is no "magical spreadsheet" that has the most recent data.

All this means we can be in the field more often!

How many beaches to break even?

We need 30 beaches a year to provide salaries and continue to grow the hammerdirt labs.

Beyond 35 beaches we would open up the opportunity to other groups. In exchange for a share of the sponsorship fee we would provide the platform, training, quality-control, manage user-accounts and data services.
