Sponsor a beach

When an organization sponsors a beach they are supporting:
  • The removal of garbage form the environment
  • Establishing a benchmark for trash levels in the region
  • The objective presentation of environmental data
  • Citizen science and constructive engagement of the public

What does the sponsor get?

Your logo, message and contact information will be visible in:
  • The data-summary table for the beach sponsored*
  • The data-summary table for the body of water where the beach is located**
  • Your message and logo will populate the Open-Graph data when shared on social media
  • One click access to all the operations and results made possible by the sponsorhip
